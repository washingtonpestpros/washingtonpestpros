Common Pests in Washington

Washington residents frequently encounter a variety of pests in both residential and commercial properties. Understanding the most common threats can help you take proactive measures:

  • Rodents: Mice and rats seeking shelter indoors during colder months
  • Ants: Carpenter ants and odorous house ants invading kitchens and wooden structures
  • Spiders: Including hobo spiders and common house spiders
  • Wasps & Hornets: Aggressive insects that build nests near roofs, patios, and eaves
  • Termites: Silent destroyers causing structural damage to homes and buildings
  • Bed Bugs: Infestations spreading through travel, apartments, and shared accommodations

Signs You Need Professional Help

Some pest infestations are obvious, while others remain hidden until damage occurs. Call Washington Pest Pros if you notice:

  • Droppings, gnaw marks, or scratching noises inside walls or attics
  • Unusual flying insects or insect trails
  • Nests, hives, or burrows around the home or yard
  • Damaged wood, mud tubes, or sagging floors (potential termites)
  • Bites, skin irritation, or allergic reactions
  • Strange odors, grease marks, or unusual stains
